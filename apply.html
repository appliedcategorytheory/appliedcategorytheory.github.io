Apply

Applications for the 2022 Adjoint School are open until January 29, 2022, 23:59 anywhere on earth. Learn about the projects for the 2022 Adjoint School here.

Application Process

To begin the application process, fill out the following pre-application form to declare your intent to apply: pre-application form.

After you fill out your pre-application, you will recieve an email with further instructions. This email will include

  1. the application form
  2. a reference form which you should send to a person who is willing to write a letter of reference on your behalf
The letter of recommendation should confirm any of the following: your background, the ACT School's relevance to your research or career, or your research or career experience.

Who should apply?

Anyone, from anywhere in the world, who is interested in applying category-theoretic methods to problems outside of pure mathematics. This is emphatically not restricted to math students, but one should be comfortable working with mathematics. Knowledge of basic category-theoretic language—the definition of monoidal category for example—is encouraged.

We will consider advanced undergraduates, PhD students, post-docs, as well as people working outside of academia. Members of groups which are underrepresented in the mathematics and computer science communities are especially encouraged to apply.
